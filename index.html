About me

Coding fascinates me. It's a problem to get stuck into and solve, to collaborate on with others or to test your own ingenuity and knowledge to their limits. It's a unique form of self expression. And it's an opportunity to contribute to a larger project with your own two (digital) hands while working alongside like-minded people. Code is all these things on top of being one of the most powerful tools we have at our disposal. To me, that's incredible.

What I do

In my spare time, I have been developing a small game in Unity. My goal was to further develop my skills with coding whilst working on a project I have passion for. I've always found practical, hands-on learning has suited me best.
